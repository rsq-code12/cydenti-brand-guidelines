04

Tone of Voice & Messaging

All Cydenti messaging must be clear, direct, and outcome-driven. No buzzwords. Identity-first, not IAM-first.

Brand Principles

🎯 Identity-First

We protect identities — human, machine, and AI. We are not an IAM, SIEM, or governance tool.

🛡️ Sovereign & Private

All AI runs in-house. No customer data leaves the platform boundary. Privacy is non-negotiable.

🤖 AI-Agent Aware

We see what others can't — autonomous agent activity, shadow AI, and LLM-driven workflows.

⚡ Outcome-Driven

Every message leads with a clear benefit. We don't sell features, we solve problems.

🔬 Modern & Technical

Slightly technical but approachable. Enterprise-grade, not academic. Think Wiz or CrowdStrike.

💎 Trustworthy

Confident, never exaggerated. No "magic AI" claims. We earn trust through clarity and honesty.

Writing Examples

✓ Do

"See every identity blind spot across your SaaS and cloud stack — in minutes."

✗ Don't

"Our revolutionary AI-powered IAM governance solution leverages cutting-edge machine learning."

✓ Do

"Cydenti detects risky permissions and shadow AI before they become incidents."

✗ Don't

"Cydenti is the world's best identity security platform with magical AI capabilities."

Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Use clear, direct sentences
  • Lead with the outcome or benefit
  • Say "Identity Security" not "IAM"
  • Mention AI agents and SaaS/cloud
  • Be confident and specific
  • Keep paragraphs short

Don't

  • Use jargon-heavy sentences
  • Over-claim "magic AI"
  • Write long, dense paragraphs
  • Position as IAM or GRC tool
  • Use buzzwords without meaning
  • Sound sales-y or hype-driven
